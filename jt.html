James Travel Website design and development, Identity design

James Travel is a travel agency at the heart of St. Petersburg, Russia. They specialize in helping with visa documents for outcoming tourists.

Website redesign and development

The target audience of a company are primarily people who travel abroad for the first time. They need a help with all of the problems and nuances that can possibly come up when they are praparing for their trip. So, James Travel do that, especially when it comes to visa documents.

My goal was to create a user-friendly website, where tourists could find all of the information they need about what kind of services James Travel provides, what types of visas they help to get and to what countries, which documents they're going to need, prices, etc.

Current website version has a responsive & adaptive design and was developed with pure HTML & CSS in April 2017 for about two weeks. And at the moment I am working on implementing some kind of CMS.

The first version of the website can be found here, in case anyone is wondering.
