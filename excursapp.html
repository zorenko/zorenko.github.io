Excursapp Mobile app, landing page and presentation design

Excursapp helps you find a perfect tour guide for a day by suggesting guides available at the moment or giving you an opportunity to book a guide in advance and providing sorting tools to find a perfect match.

Learn more on excursapp.com

You can play with an Invision prototype to experience what it might look like.

School project

While I was studying at HSE Art & Design School I came up with the idea of Excursapp for my finals. For the Demo Day I designed a presentation for this project.
