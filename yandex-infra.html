Dark theme

The goal was to update a color scheme for light and a11y themes of the platform and create a dark theme.

The UI of a platform used too many similar colours (several greens, reds, etc.), plus some colours weren't very user-friendly (like green was too bright). That was a legacy from a lot of previous designers who worked on a platform before. Colours created problems for both users and developers. I needed to systemise, reduce and update a color palette.

While I was working on a task, I took several colours-rich screens of the platform and started to look for patterns, similarities and differences.

In the end I've created a big palette of old colours that were used in the UI and started systemise and enhance them. By the end of this process I've collected a new updated palette.

We worked closely with developers and some users and they consulted me on where some colours might not work and what decisions might confuse platform users.

After getting together light theme color palette I've started working on dark theme. When it was done, we've announced that color update in Yandex internal blog system. In general, YT users were very happy with an update and excited about dark theme.
